WHO
WE ARE

Established in 2004, Ultrasound specialize in lighting control and automation systems that are
designed to make your life easier. We use the latest technology to make sure that our
products are reliable and efficient. Our team has over 30 years of experience in the industry,
and we are dedicated to helping our clients find the best solution for their needs.
We offer a wide range of services, from custom installations to automated lighting control
systems. We understand that each client has unique requirements and we work hard to
ensure that our services are tailored to their specific needs. With our vast industry knowledge
and experience, we guarantee that our clients are getting the best service and products
available.
